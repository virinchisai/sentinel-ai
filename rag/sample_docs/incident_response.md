# Incident Response Runbook (Summary)

Any production incident affecting customer-facing services must be declared in
the #incidents Slack channel and tagged with a severity level (SEV1-SEV3). SEV1
incidents require paging the on-call engineer immediately and notifying the
engineering director. A post-incident review (postmortem) is required within 5
business days of resolution for SEV1 and SEV2 incidents, documenting root cause,
timeline, and follow-up action items.
