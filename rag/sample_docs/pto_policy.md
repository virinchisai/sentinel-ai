# PTO Policy

Full-time employees accrue 15 days of paid time off (PTO) per year, accrued monthly
at a rate of 1.25 days/month. PTO requests must be submitted at least 2 weeks in
advance via the HR portal for approval by your manager. Unused PTO carries over up
to a maximum of 5 days into the next calendar year; any balance beyond that is
forfeited on December 31st. Sick leave is tracked separately and does not draw
down the PTO balance.
