# New Hire Onboarding FAQ

New hires receive a laptop and equipment kit shipped to their home address before
their start date. IT access (email, Slack, GitHub) is provisioned automatically
once HR completes the new-hire paperwork, typically within 24 hours of the offer
being signed. All employees must complete security awareness training within
their first 7 days. Your manager is responsible for assigning a 30-60-90 day
onboarding plan during your first week.
